# Unit 05 — Exercises (مشق)

Each lesson ends with a مشق. The unit drills the three plural pronouns and two plural
demonstratives through the same cycle as Unit 4, plus sound-plural formation and the
"famous expressions". Recurring types:

1. **جمع بنائیں** — Form the sound plural (masc. ـُونَ/ـِينَ, fem. ـَات) of a given word,
   e.g. مُسْلِم → مُسْلِمُونَ / مُسْلِمِينَ ؛ مُؤْمِنَة → مُؤْمِنَات.
2. **ضمیر کا مطلب / لگائیں** — State the meaning of a plural pronoun/suffix, or attach it
   (هُمْ، أَنْتُمْ، نَحْنُ ؛ ـهُمْ، ـكُمْ، ـنَا).
3. **حرف کے ساتھ ضمیر** — Join a suffix to a particle (لِـ → لَهُمْ / لَكُمْ / لَنَا ؛
   مِنْ → مِنْهُمْ / مِنْكُمْ / مِنَّا).
4. **قرآنی جملوں کا ترجمہ** — Translate the Quranic phrases/āyāt (all in `ayaat.csv`).
5. **نمایاں کریں** — Highlight the pronoun, demonstrative, or expression (هَلْ، أَلَا،
   أَلَيْسَ) in each āyah.

The pronoun forms and sound-plural examples are in `vocabulary.csv`; the rules and
examples in `grammar.md`; the Quranic verses in `ayaat.csv`.
